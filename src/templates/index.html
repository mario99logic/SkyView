Celestial Choreography: Interactive Exploration of the Cosmos

Explore the marvels of the universe with "Celestial Choreography", an immersive web application that combines classical physics with cutting-edge computational techniques to simulate and visualize the dynamic movements of celestial bodies. Our application allows users to interactively build models of the solar system, track the motion of planets, and observe celestial events through a Pygame simulation window. Additionally, delve into a daily dose of cosmic wonder with NASA's Image of the Day feature, and seamlessly search for NASA's vast array of celestial data. Whether you're a student, educator, or astronomy enthusiast, Celestial Choreography offers a unique platform to connect with the cosmos like never before.
