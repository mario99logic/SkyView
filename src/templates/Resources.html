Educational Resources

SkyView simulations and interactives are designed to be used easily for a variety of purposes within the informal learning setting.

Exhibit Space

SkyView simulations can be incorporated into formal and informal exhibit spaces throughout your center.

  • Use the SkyView collection as a stand-alone exhibit.
  • Incorporate individual simulations into astronomy and Earth science exhibits.
  • Set up SkyView in a lobby, caf√©, waiting area, library, or children's play area.
  • Support the SkyView exhibit with banners and labels.

Facilitated Activities

SkyView can be used as a primary or supplemental resource for presentations, tours, and exploration stations.

  • Incorporate SkyView content into formal presentations for engagement, exploration, or elaboration.
  • Set up SkyView on a tablet or laptop as part of an exploration station or cart.
  • Equip multiple tablets or laptops with SkyView at various stations to allow visitors to explore at their own pace, each one making his own model.
  • Support the SkyView exhibit with banners and labels.

Educational Programming

SkyView can be used in multiple ways with school groups of a wide range of ages.

  • SkyView simulations can be used within presentations, discussions, and activities.
  • Supply teachers with direct links to built models for classroom use before or after their visit.
  • Demonstrate SkyView and provide students with the link to extend their learning experience at home.
